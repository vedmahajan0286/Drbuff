What We Do

Exterior and Interior Washing

Keeping your car washed is a best way to protect the value and maintain appearance. Contaminants such as dirt, pollen, tree sap, air pollutants and deceased bugs can all damage the great look and paint of your car.

Ceramic Coating

Ceramic Coating acts as a strong protective layer above your paintwork, protecting it from UV rays and environmental contaminants. Just a thin coat of the coating and it forms an extremely hard shell on the car’s surface. What’s even better is that it also creates a beautiful shine on your car exterior

Vacuum Cleaning

DR.Buff ensures that the interior of your vehicle including places that are hard to reach is properly cleaned. If you love riding the car every day, you need to ensure that it is kept clean from inside too.

Seats Washing

With all car seats, prevention is better than cure, DR.Buff advise that spending a little time for keeping your car seats clean and hygienic will pay off in the long run

Engine Coating

This treatment is designed to provide a high gloss and slippery coating on the engine parts, hoses, etc. The primary advantage of this treatment is to prevent the hoses and electrical wiring from getting bitten by rats so that a vehicle parked for a long duration is not a victim of such untoward incidents

Glass Treatment

This is for cleansing and protection of windshield of cars. With this treatment, hard water marks and oxidation are removed and a fine protective layer is formed to provide clear vision for driving during the night as well as during the rainy season. This enhances the safety & drive-ability

Headlight Restoration

New bulbs might restore some headlight brightness, But clouded lenses that aren’t restored will continue to reduce headlight effectiveness, potentially causing safety issues in nighttime driving and low-visibility conditions.

Under Body Coating

As underbody is subjected to sever exposure to dust, moisture. It would result in faster deterioration of the coating done at factory. Getting antirust coating at dealership would provide additional protection and assures longer life of the components

AC Duct Cleaning

A clean ventilation system helps keep smog, allergens, and other pollutants from entering your car’s cabin.Regular cleaning of the evaporator core, which is a type of heat exchange device, can help to facilitate proper heating and cooling of a vehicle’s interior. A clean core can help to facilitate the refrigerant to properly absorb heat and then convert it to a low-temperature vapor within the evaporator coils.

Car And Bike Detailing

Vehicle detailing is a step-by-step process performed to restore & revive the dull and faded surfaces of vehicles back to a brand new finish.Unlike washing the exterior of your vehicle with soapy water and a rag, or swiping the interior with water and vacuuming it
